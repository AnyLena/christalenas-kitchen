Labskaus

Quantities Ingredients
340 g Canned Corned Beef
1 kg Potatoes
500 g Pickled Red Beet
2 Bay Leaves
3 Onions
4 Gherkins
2 tbsp Liquid from the Gherkins
some Rapeseed Oil, Salt and Pepper
1 Pinch Ground Cloves
4 Eggs
4 Matjes Fillets

Instructions

  • Cook the peeled and chopped potatoes with the bay leaves in salted water.
  • Sauté the finely diced onions in oil for about 10 minutes over a medium heat, add the corned beef, cut into pieces and heat through. Dice half of the pickled beet and sauté, then pour in a little cucumber liquid. Leave everything to simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Heat the vegetable stock in a separate pan and add the cooked potatoes. Roughly mash everything into mashed potatoes. Then stir in the meat, beetroot and onion mixture, season everything with the spices and keep warm.
  • Now fry the four eggs.
  • To serve, arrange the Labskaus mash with a fried egg, a matjes fillet, some beetroot and gherkins.
