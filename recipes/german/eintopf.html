Gemüseeintopf mit Mettenden

Quantities Ingredients
1 bunch Greens
1 bunch Parsley
1–2 Onions
2 Bay Leaves
600 g Potaoes
500 g Carrots
450 g Leek
250 g Frozen or Canned Peas
to tase Salt, Pepper, Vegetable Stock Powder
1-2 per Person Mettwürste (Sausages)

Instructions

  • Clean, wash and roughly chop the soup greens. Pluck the parsley leaves, put the stalks to one side. Wash and cut the onions. Roast the onions in a large pan without fat.
  • Pour in 2 liters of water, add the greens, parsley stalks, bay leaves, 1 teaspoon of salt, some pepper. Bring to the boil, cover and simmer for approx. 30 minutes.
  • Peel, wash and dice the potatoes. Wash, peel and slice the carrots. Clean and wash the leek and cut into rings. Chop the parsley.
  • Add the potato, carrots, leek and Mettwurst, cover and cook for approx. 15 minutes. Approx. 3 minutes before the end of the cooking time, add the frozen peas and simmer.
  • Add the parsley to the soup, except for a little for sprinkling, and season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with the remaining parsley.
