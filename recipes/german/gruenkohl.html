Grünkohl mit Pinkel

Quantities Ingredients
2,5 kg Kale
1 Onion
100 g Smoked Pancetta
2 tbsp Lard
250 g Pork Belly Meat
500 ml Vegetable Stock
5 Pinkel Sausages
1 tsp Mustard

Instructions

  • Remove the stems from the kale, wash and drain. Place the kale in a large pan of boiling salted water. Allow to collapse, drain in a sieve and then roughly chop.
  • Peel and finely chop the onion. Dice the bacon. Heat the lard in a pan and sauté the onion until translucent. Add the bacon and pork belly and fry.
  • Add the kale and pour in approx. 300 ml vegetable stock. Place the pork belly between the kale and cook for approx. 1 hour. Add a little stock every now and then.
  • After an hour's cooking time, put Pinkel sausage into the kale and mix the contents. Simmer for another 5 minutes, season with salt and mustard.
  • Remove the pork belly, cut into pieces and serve with the kale and sausages. Serve with boiled potatoes and mustard.
