Souvlaki

Quantities Ingredients
400gr Pork tenderloin
4tbp White wine
1 piece Onion
4 pieces Pitas
1 piece Tomatoe
with the eye Salt / Pepper / Oil
200gr Tzatziki
200gr French Fries

Instructions

  • Remove the white membrane covering the top side of the meat with a sharp knife. It is very important to remove this membrane because no matter how long you cook it, it will always be tough.Cut the meat into equal, thin slanted slices that are 0.5 cm thick.Transfer meat to a deep pan.
  • Thinly slice an onion and add it to the pan. Add the olive oil, 5 tablespoons vinegar, salt, pepper and honey. Mix with a wooden spoon to coat. (Do not add more honey because it will give too much color to the meat when it is cooking and it will look burned when it won’t be. Also, when you add the marinade to the meat, you will notice that it will start to turn white. This is because of the vinegar so don’t let it worry you.)
  • Let a non-stick pan get very hot over high heat, to the point where it is smoking. When it is hot enough, add 2 tablespoons olive oil and add the meat in 3 batches. If you overcrowd the pan, the temperature in the pan will drop and the meat will boil instead of brown nicely. When ready, add 1 tablespoon vinegar and stir. Remove from pan and continue with the next batch.
  • Place the gyro on the pitas and add tomatoes, potatoes, tzatziki sauce or any other combination of toppings you like!
