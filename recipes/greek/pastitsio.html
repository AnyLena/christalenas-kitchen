Pastitsio

Quantities Ingredients
300gr Bucatini pasta
100ml Red wine
1 piece Onion
1 clove Garlic
500ml Bechamel
with the eye Salt / Pepper / Oil
500 g ground beef
400g canned tomatoes

Instructions

  • Place a deep pan over high heat. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, the finely chopped onions, 1 tablespoon granulated sugar and finely chopped garlic. Sauté for 3-4 minutes, until the onions caramelize nicely.
  • Add the ground meat. Break it up with a wooden spoon and brown for 4-5 minutes. At this point you may need to add another 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Add the wine and let the alcohol evaporate. Add the canned tomatoes, cinnamon, sugar, bouillon cubes and bay leaves.
  • Turn the heat down to low and simmer for 10-20 minutes, until the sauce reduces and the ground meat is cooked.
  • Add the pasta to a pot full of salted water. Boil 1-2 minutes less than the instructions on the box. They need to be al dente because they will cook further in the oven, so do not overcook.
  • Preheat oven to 180* C (350* F) Fan. Bring back the baking pan containing the pasta mixture. To the ground meat, add a ladleful of the béchamel sauce. Mix until completely incorporated. Spread the ground meat mixture over the pasta in the baking pan.
  • Bake for 40 minutes or until the béchamel turns golden brown.
